meet Yosola

Meet Yosola, a disciple of Jesus, keynote speaker, business consultant, entrepreneur, and transformational coach. She holds a Bachelor's in marine biology and Master's degrees in marine pollution, community health, and business. With over a decade of diverse work experience, she's founded Purpose Club, Foodpreneurs Hub, and Veenode Technologies. Yosola's unique ability to clarify ambiguity helps aspiring entrepreneurs and corporate executives succeed. Her journey from academia to entrepreneurship is an inspiration, while her commitment to purpose and community drives her impact on the African business landscape.
